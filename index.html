The Start
March 25, 2026

Dear Team,

Why we exist

Every single day, hundreds of millions of Indians get on the road. They deal with chaos, danger, uncertainty — and they do it without a single intelligent system looking out for them. No guidance. No protection. No record of what happened when things go wrong. That is the problem we are here to solve.

Arambh.ai exists to transform Indian road experiences. Not improve. Not optimise. Transform. We will give consumers security, confidence and guidance. We will give businesses the intelligence they need — Incident Management, Driver Profiles, Traffic Monitoring — systems that make the road smarter for everyone on it.

We start with a dashcam. But make no mistake — this is a platform. This is infrastructure. This is the kind of thing that, ten years from now, people will say changed how India moves.

I believe this project has the X factor to do something crazy. The kind of crazy that matters.

Where we stand today

I'm not going to dress this up. Technically, we have nothing yet but the vision. The GitHub organisation is live. We're talking to the right people. We're learning how to build a startup the right way — and we're implementing as we go. That's it. That's where we are.

And here's the thing — that's exactly where every company that ever mattered once stood. Before the product. Before the funding. Before the users. There was just a small group of people who chose to believe early, while it was still uncomfortable to do so.

You are that group. You are here at the beginning — and that means something profound. The people in this room right now will one day be the origin story.

The honest part

Right now, before we raise funding, we are all working on this part time — me included. I know that. I respect that. And I am not asking you to give what you don't have. What I am asking is simpler: give what you can, when you can, and make it count.

The dream we're chasing is big enough to deserve your best moments — not just your spare ones. So whenever you show up for Arambh, show up fully. One great contribution this week is worth more than ten half-hearted ones.

How we build together

There are no fixed lanes here. No waiting to be told what to do. If you see something that needs doing — a problem to solve, a task to list, a question to answer — pick it up. Self-assign. Move. Right now, the act of listing things to do is itself a contribution. The act of showing up and saying "I'll take that" is leadership.

Ownership is everything at Arambh. Not because we demand it — but because the people who own things are the ones who build things. Once you pick something up, it's yours. See it through. Don't let it sit. Ownership questioned is ownership lost — and we will never be that kind of team.

We are building a culture right now, in these early days, whether we intend to or not. Every action, every commitment, every moment of initiative — that's what Arambh will be made of. Let's make it something worth being proud of.

What I need from you — today

Don't wait for the product to be ready. Don't wait for funding. Don't wait for someone to tell you the perfect moment to contribute. The perfect moment is now. Open the GitHub. Find a task. Write something down. Talk to someone who should know about what we're building. Do one thing today that moves Arambh one step forward.

Because here's what I know to be true: startups are not built by grand gestures. They are built by small acts of courage, repeated every day, by people who refused to wait until they were certain.

The road is long. The curve is steep. The work is real.

But the story we're writing — the one that transforms how a billion people experience the road — is worth every bit of it.

This is Arambh. This is the beginning. Let's build.

With belief, honesty, and a great deal of excitement,
Shailendra Sekhar
CEO & Co-founder, Arambh.ai
